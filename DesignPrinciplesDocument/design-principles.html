White Space

Google

www.google.com/autos

The space between each word creates distinction. Each line of text is separated with just enough space to indicate a relationship with the text above and below. Even more space is added between paragraphs to indicate that a new idea or point is being made.

Alignment

Epec

www.epec.com.ar

Vertical alignment gets it name, not because of the invisible horizontal lines, but because of the vertical space within the element being aligned with another. One trick to remember this is to think of the direction you go when drawing a line from top to bottom. It’s not always necessary or possible for a row of elements to be vertically aligned by their tops, bottoms AND center. In many cases, your elements will have variable heights and you’ll have to decide which of these makes the most sense.

Contrast in web design can be used to highlight the elements that we are most interested in that the user perceives clearly. Contrast is called the differences between two related elements and is one of the principles of web design. In this case between the backgrpund and the text-colors
