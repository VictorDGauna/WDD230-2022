Concern over new greenhouse gas record

The World Meteorological Organization (WMO ) has announced a new record for greenhouse gases in 2020 , exceeding the annual rate recorded in the average for the period 2011-2020. This has been communicated by the United Nations body in its annual report on greenhouse gases for the last 12 months. They claim that the amount of Co2 , the most important greenhouse gas, has reached an average of 413 parts per million in the atmosphere, with peaks of 420 . It must be remembered that only five years ago they did not exceed 400. Clima.com
